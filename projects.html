Projects

As a student changemaker, I have initiated and led a wide variety of projects.

Tech Connect

Tech Connect is an extracurricular activity, which exposes students to unique applications of technology related to STEM topics and encourages them to think differently, connect differently and snatch opportunities coming their way. Through the club, students have a chance to develop crucial skills like critical thinking, decision making, curiosity, problem solving, leadership, innovation, acceptance of failure etc., enabling them to become more successful individuals in the future. Additionally, they develop a love of STEM and grow to be interested in STEM subjects and jobs in the future.

Technovation Girls Student Ambassador

Being selected to act as a champion of Technovation Girls for this region, I feel that it is crucial to empower girls to change their perception of themselves, so that they view themselves as not only the users of technology, but as inventors, designers and entrepreneurs. The competition focuses on encouraging girls to solve real-world problems through technology. Through the competition, girls can get an insight into the problems faced by people in other countries, as well as interact with others on a global level. With this role, I am have recruited participants and am currently hosting events to explain my first-hand experience of participating in the competition. I will be motivating and supporting participants throughout the program and recruiting girls from underserved communities as well.

ESF CoCo 2022 Core Team

The ESF Computing Conference is an event consisting of interactive workshops by renowned industry leaders, a hackathon and an e-sports competiton. The conference aims to foster and encourage a love for computer science, starting from a young age. As a part of the core team, I will be involved in contacting industry leaders, liasing with ESF and planning the hackathon and e-sports competition.
